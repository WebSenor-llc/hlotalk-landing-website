Safety Commitment

Child Safety

HloTalk is committed to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

01 Our Prohibitions

Our platform strictly prohibits:

  • Any content involving child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Exploitation, grooming, or harassment of minors
  • Sharing, storing, or promoting illegal or harmful content involving minors

02 Safety Measures

  • Users can report abusive behavior and content directly within the app.
  • Reported content is reviewed promptly by our moderation team.
  • We take immediate action including removing content, suspending accounts, and reporting to relevant law enforcement authorities when required.
  • We cooperate fully with regional and national authorities in CSAE investigations.

03 User Responsibility

Users must comply with our safety policies and applicable laws. Any violations will result in strict enforcement actions.

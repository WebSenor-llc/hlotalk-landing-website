Legal & Security

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. This document explains how we collect, use, and protect your data at hlotalk.

01 Information We Collect

At hlotalk, we collect specific information to ensure you get the best possible experience on our platform. This collection includes:

  • Account details: Basic information such as your chosen username and registered email.
  • Profile contents: Any photos, bio text, or multimedia you choose to upload.
  • Matching preferences: Your interests and search criteria used to find relevant matches.
  • Usage data: Technical logs and app interaction patterns to help us improve performance.

02 How We Use Information

Your data is used solely to enhance the hlotalk experience. We keep your information private and never sell your data to third-party advertisers.

  • To connect you with people nearby based on your shared interests.
  • To optimize app performance and introduce new meaningful features.
  • To maintain the safety of our community by detecting fraudulent activity.

03 Data Security

Security is integrated into every part of hlotalk. We utilize end-to-end encryption for all audio and video calls, ensuring your private moments stay private. All data is stored on high-security servers with industry-standard protection protocols.

04 Your Rights

You have complete control over your information. You can access, update, or permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time through the in-app settings or by contacting our support team.

05 Updates to This Policy

As we evolve, we may update this policy to reflect new features or regulations. We will always notify you of significant changes through the app or registered email.

Our Story

Connecting the world through real conversations.

At hlotalk, we're on a mission to bring back the human touch to socializing. We prioritize voice and video because true connection happens when you hear a laugh or see a smile.

Our Mission

We created Hlotalk to solve a problem: modern socializing is often shallow. Swiping through endless profiles can feel disconnected. Our mission is to normalize genuine audio and video conversations.

Who We Are

We're a team of designers, engineers, and community builders who believe that social apps should be more than just algorithms. We prioritize privacy, safety, and authenticity above all else.

Our Story

Starting in 2024 as a small experiment, we noticed people were lonely despite being "connected." By focusing on real-time talk, we've helped thousands find genuine friendships.

Join Our Journey

Whether you're looking for a new friend or just someone to talk to, Hlotalk is here for you. We're constantly working to improve and would love for you to be part of our community.
